What is Blockchain?

Essentially Blockchain is a immutable record keeping ledger. or in other words, A record keeping device that is used to track and manage asset ownership.
Triple entry ledger. 

Blockchain leverages three older technologies
1.Ledger: record keeping system ex.(bank account)
2.Cryptography: Getting information back and forth in the presence of different entities.
3.P2P(Peer2peer):Gives individuals the ability to interact directly without a third party or middleman.
